Frientrip

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 16, 2026

Overview

Frientrip ("we", "our", or "us") is a group trip planning app that helps friends coordinate travel, costs, and logistics. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

  • Account info — your name and email address when you register or sign in with Google
  • Trip data — trip names, dates, costs, supply lists, carpool info, and nights stayed that you enter
  • Profile data — display name and avatar used within your trip groups

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To sync trip data across devices and with other trip members
  • To send trip invitations to other users by email
  • To calculate and display shared costs among trip members

Data Sharing

Trip data (names, costs, nights, supplies) is shared with other members of your trip group. Your email address may be visible to trip owners when you are invited to a trip.

We do not sell your data to third parties, ever.

Third-Party Services

Frientrip uses the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Firestore database, and file storage
  • Google Sign-In — Optional sign-in method

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Trip data is retained as long as the trip exists in the app.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@frientrip.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Frientrip is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@frientrip.app.
